# Welcome to Danmaek

Welcome to **Danmaek** — your bilingual news hub for Korean and English content.

Danmaek (단맥) is short for "protein beer" (단백질 맥주), a personal project by proteinbeer to curate and compare news from Korea and the English-speaking world.

## What to expect

- **Korean news** translated and contextualized for English readers
- **English news** with Korean perspectives
- **Side-by-side comparisons** of how the same story is covered in different markets

Stay tuned for regular updates!
